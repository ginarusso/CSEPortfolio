About me

I'm a Fullstack Web Developer, Computer Science Educator, School Library Media & Educational Technology Specialist. I believe coding isn't just for computer programmers... it's a literacy - one of the basic fundamentals of learning! I enjoy teaching students through an engaging, hands-on approach using microcontrollers, servo motors, light emitting diodes, breadboards, resistors, and other components. When students learn about physical computing, they are building the foundational skills needed to thrive in a competitive world.
